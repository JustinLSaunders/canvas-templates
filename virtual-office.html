This discussion forum serves as a virtual space to post questions and comments about course concepts and assignments. It's a place to publicly discuss what's on your mind and any concerns you may have, just as you would in a physical student lounge on campus.

Participation in this message board is optional. I will be checking this discussion forum daily or when posts are made within 24 hours on weekdays and within 48 hours on the weekend.

If you have a private matter you wish to discuss, or have questions regarding your grades, please send it directly to me via email.

To post either a text or video message, click the box labeled  Reply at the bottom of this page. You can either type your response or select the  Record/Upload Media icon to record a short video using your webcam. You also have options to provide a link, embed an image, or attach a file. Click the blue Post Reply button to submit your post.

I encourage you to respond to your classmates and even to return to your own comments once you have engaged with course activities and resources.
