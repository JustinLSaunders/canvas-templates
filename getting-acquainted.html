As a way of introduction, please answer the prompts below. To begin, click the box labeled  Reply at the bottom of this page. You can either type your response or select the Record/Upload Media icon to record a short video using your webcam. Click the blue Post Reply button to submit your post. After you have contributed, return to this page to greet your classmates and respond to any comments that may have been added to your post. You should feel free to make additional comments and continue the conversations that emerge.

Detailed Instructions

This is your space to introduce yourself to the class. In your introduction post, please include answers to the following questions:

[NOTE TO INSTRUCTIONAL DESIGNER: These are just boilerplate questions. We encourage the faculty to write their own.]

  • What is your name?
  • Where are you joining from geographically? (New York City or elsewhere in the US or abroad?)
  • What is your program of study?
  • Are you currently working in addition to taking classes, or were you working before starting your program of study?
  • Do you plan to pursue an additional advanced degree, and if so, in what field of study?
  • How do you hope to apply what you learn in this course and program in your future career?
  • Please let us know a little something more about your interests or hobbies.

Please post your introduction before our first Class Session. Once you have posted your introduction, please check back throughout the week to greet your classmates. If possible, please make all additional comments to this page before the end of the first week.
