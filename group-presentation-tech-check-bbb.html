This activity is designed to familiarize you with a video-conferencing tool, BigBlueButton, which you will use to facilitate and record a video group assignment in the coming modules. Additionally, BigBlueButton will be used for portions of your final assignment in this course [NOTE TO INSTRUCTIONAL DESIGNER: Adapt this language as suits your course].

Here, you have the opportunity to practice using the tool, in a relatively low-stakes activity. Because the conferencing technology may be unfamiliar to you, we want to alleviate any concerns your team may have with it prior to the critical recording session for your group assignment.

For this activity, your team will prepare a conference, perform a sound and video check, record a brief introduction—no longer than a minute, and submit the URL to your recording.

Details

Along with your fellow group member(s), record a one-minute-long video in BigBlueButton, via the Conferences tool in your Canvas group. Your video does not need to be complicated—a simple sound and video check along with a greeting to the instructor will do—but each group member should appear on webcam and speak.

PLEASE NOTE: To record a conference, you MUST select the "Enable recording for this conference" option when you initially create the conference. Otherwise, once the conference has started, it will not record, even if you go back and edit the conference.

You will record your tech check using Conferences inside your group work space. Click Groups in the Canvas global navigation menu to locate your group. Designate one member of the group to be responsible for creating the conferences under the Conferences link in the Group navigation.

Please carefully review the Canvas guide: How do I record a conference in a student group?

After you have completed your conference, the recording will take a little time to prepare (please be patient). Once the recording has finished rendering, you will be able to view it in the Conferences area of your Canvas group in the Concluded Conferences section. Feel free to review your recording.

Submission

Only one member of your group needs to submit the recording. Follow the below steps to submit the assignment:

  1. View your concluded conference in your Canvas group.
  2. Copy the URL (web address) of your recorded conference. It should begin with http://recordings.blindsidenetworks.com.
  3. Click the blue Submit Assignment button at the top of this assignment page.
  4. Paste or type the URL of your recorded conference in the Website URL field.
  5. Feel free to include a comment with your submission.
  6. Finally, click Submit Assignment.

Assessment

This activity is graded as complete/incomplete. If you successfully submit your BigBlueButton recording, you will receive full credit.
