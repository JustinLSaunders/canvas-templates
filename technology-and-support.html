Piazza

Access Piazza by clicking on Piazza on the left hand-navigation in your Canvas course. Log into Piazza by signing in with the password you create the first time you sign in.

  • Instructions for the first time you sign into Piazza
  • You will use Piazza as the main discussion forum for the course. It is a Q&A wiki for your class. Every question has a single student's answer that other classmates can edit collectively.
  • To comment on or ask further questions about a post, start a followup discussion. Mark it resolved when the issue has been addressed.
  • Click the Fx button to access the LaTeX editor to type in nicely formatted math equations.

In the Telling

In The Telling is a transmedia storytelling player that will present your course lectures along with time-stamped exercises as the media plays back. The player allows the course media to be played back in a unified container as part of the course modules. Here you will be able to:

  • Watch your course lectures and other media in a unified container.
  • Launch and participate in Discussions based on a video or media.
  • Launch and submit Assignments based on the video or media.

In The Telling requires an HTML5 compatible browser on both Windows or Mac operating systems. Download the In the Telling Player Instructions
