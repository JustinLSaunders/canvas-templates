The purpose of this assignment is to guarantee that your webcams and microphones function correctly and that you are fully familiar with how to record in and submit a presentation from the video-conference tool Zoom. It should reduce any anxiety related to preparation for recording a video presentation.

Details

In preparation for your Final Group Presentation, students will record a brief video in Zoom. The video does not need to be substantial and should be no longer than a minute. The objective of this technology check is to troubleshoot any issues early on.

  1. Your team Host should navigate to zoom, and sign in. Schedule a meeting and email your team members the log in details.
  2. Log in to the Zoom meeting URL that your team Host provided at the time of your meeting.
  3. Start recording and take turns so that each team member appears on webcam, says hello, and introduces themselves.
  4. The group member who will be sharing their screen during the final presentation should do a brief screen share (you can show anything on your screen; the point is to practice this for the final presentation)
  5. Stop the recording and submit your assignment.

Zoom

You will use Zoom, a video-conferencing tool, to deliver your final presentation. There are several resources available to familiarize you with the use of Zoom.

  • Download the Zoom App
  • Host guide for scheduling your personal meeting room.
  • Specific guides for each function:
    • How Do I Join Or Test My Computer Audio?
    • How Do I Test My Video?
    • How Do I Share My Screen?

  • Zoom Help Center's Getting Started Guides, including Frequently Asked Questions.
  • Additional online Canvas guides:
    Files | Collaborations | Conferences

Submission

Only one member of your group needs to submit the recording. Follow the below steps to submit the assignment:

  1. Copy the URL (web address) of your recorded conference.
  2. Click the blue Submit Assignment button at the top of this page.
  3. Paste or type the URL of your recorded conference in the Website URL field.
  4. Feel free to include a comment with your submission.
  5. Finally, click the blue Submit Assignment button at the bottom of this page.

Assessment

This activity is graded as complete/incomplete. If you successfully submit your recording, you will receive full credit.
