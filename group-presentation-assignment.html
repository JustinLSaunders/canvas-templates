Recording Your Group Presentation

Using BigBlueButton

There are several guides available to familiarize you with Canvas groups and BigBlueButton. If you have not recorded a BigBlueButton conference before, please carefully review the resources below.

  • How do I view my Canvas groups as a student?
  • How do I use the Conferences interface as a participant?
  • How do I create a conference in a student group?
  • How do I record a conference in a student group?
  • Additional online Canvas guides:
    Files | Collaborations | Conferences

After you have completed your conference, the recording will take a little time to prepare (please be patient). Once the recording has finished rendering, you will be able to view it in the Conferences area of your Canvas group in the Concluded Conferences section. Feel free to review your recording.

Submission

To submit your group video-conference recording video,

  1. View your concluded conference in your Canvas group.
  2. Copy the URL (web address) of your recorded conference. It should begin with http://recordings.blindsidenetworks.com.
  3. Click the blue Submit Assignment button at the top of this page.
  4. Paste or type the URL of your recorded conference in the Website URL field.
  5. Feel free to include a comment with your submission.
  6. Finally, click the blue Submit Assignment button at the bottom of this page.
